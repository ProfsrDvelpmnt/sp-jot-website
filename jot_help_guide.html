üöÄ Getting Started Guide

Creating Your Account

  1. Navigate to app.sp-jot.com in your web browser
  2. Click the "Sign Up" button
  3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the confirmation link
  5. Complete your profile setup, including your name and job search preferences
Pro Tip: Use a professional email address that you check regularly to ensure you don't miss important notifications about your job applications.

Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, your personalized dashboard displays several key sections:

Quick Statistics

  • Active Jobs: Shows the number of job applications currently in progress
  • Interviews This Week: Displays upcoming interviews scheduled
  • Applications This Month: Tracks submissions in the current month
  • Overall Growth: Progress indicator showing your journey
  • Extension Usage: Tracks your JOT Snatcher browser extension usage

Quick Actions

  • Add & Update Jobs: Click "Manage Your Jobs" to track all applications
  • View Calendar: Click "View Your Week" to see upcoming interviews and deadlines
  • Check Archive: Click "View Archive" to review closed jobs

Adding Your First Job Application

  1. From the Dashboard, click "Manage Your Jobs" or navigate to Board in the left sidebar
  2. Click the "Add Job" button in the top right corner
  3. Fill in the job details:
    • Job Title
    • Company Name
    • Location
    • Salary Range
    • Job Type (Full Time, Part-Time, etc.)
    • Environment (Remote, In-Person, Hybrid)
    • Source (LinkedIn, Indeed, Company Website, etc.)
  4. Add any additional notes or details
  5. The job will automatically be placed in the appropriate status column

Navigating the Main Menu

The left sidebar contains your main navigation, divided into three sections:

MAIN Section

  • Dashboard: Your overview and quick statistics
  • Calendar: Schedule and track interviews and deadlines
  • Board: Visual kanban board of all your applications
  • Profile: Manage your personal information

MINDFULNESS Section

  • Daily Check-In: Track your daily job search mindset
  • Gratitude Journal: Record things you're grateful for
  • Goal Setting: Set and track your job search goals
  • Rejection Reframe: Transform rejection into learning opportunities
  • Values Assessment: Identify what matters most in your career

PREMIUM GROWTH Section

  • Growth: Access premium features for tracking progress across mindfulness activities

üìä Kanban Board Tutorial

What is the JOT Board?

The JOT Board is a visual kanban board that helps you organize and track your job applications through different stages. It provides a clear visual representation of your entire job search pipeline.

Understanding the Pipeline Stages

Your job applications move through the following stages:

  • Saved: Jobs you're interested in applying to later; your "watchlist"
  • Applying: Jobs you're currently applying to but haven't submitted yet
  • Applied: Applications you've submitted, awaiting response
  • Contacted: Employers who have reached out but haven't scheduled an interview yet
  • Interviewing: Active interview processes, including all interview rounds
  • Offer: Jobs where you've received a formal job offer
  • Negotiating: Applications where you're actively negotiating terms, salary, or benefits
  • Hired: Jobs where you've accepted an offer and started working
  • Archived: Closed or past applications (available in higher tier plans)

Note: Stage access varies by subscription plan.

Job Card Details

Each job card displays comprehensive information:

  • Job Title (blue/cyan text at the top)
  • Company Name
  • Location and job type details
  • Salary Range
  • Employment Type
  • Environment (Remote, Hybrid, In-Person)
  • Source (where you found the job)
  • Age of Posting

Application Timeline

Track your progress with visual indicators:

  • Saved: Date when you saved the job (calendar icon)
  • Applying: Date you started the application
  • Applied: Date you submitted the application (checkmark icon)
  • Contacted: Date the employer reached out
  • Interviewed: Date of interview (checkmark when completed)

Moving Jobs Between Stages

  1. Locate the job card in its current stage
  2. Click and drag the card to the new stage column
  3. Drop it into the appropriate section
  4. The status and timeline will automatically update

Card Actions

  • Prev/Next: Navigate between related applications or stages
  • Edit icon (pencil): Modify job details
  • Copy icon: Duplicate the job entry
  • Delete icon (trash): Remove the job from your tracker

Top Bar Controls

  • Archive: View and manage archived jobs
  • Export: Export your job data
  • Refresh: Reload the board with latest data
  • Add Job: Create a new job entry
Pro Tip: Keep your board organized by archiving jobs you've decided not to pursue or that have been closed for more than 30 days.

üìÖ Calendar Features

Calendar Overview

The JOT Activities Calendar helps you track your job search activities and important deadlines. Access it by clicking Calendar in the left sidebar under the MAIN section.

Calendar Views

The calendar offers four distinct viewing options:

Agenda View

  • Presents a detailed schedule for an individual date
  • Includes a "Jobs for [Date]" section
  • Ideal for focusing on specific days

Day View

  • Displays a single day broken down into hourly time slots
  • Perfect for detailed daily planning

Week View

  • Shows the entire week at a glance
  • Displays date range (e.g., "Sep 28 - Oct 4, 2025")
  • Best for weekly planning and comparing activities

Month View

  • Shows a full calendar grid covering the entire month
  • Includes toggle for "1 Month" or "3 Months" views
  • Excellent for broad, long-term planning

Calendar Navigation

  • Use the Previous and Next buttons to move backward or forward in time
  • Click tabs at the top to switch between Agenda, Day, Week, and Month views
  • In Month View, toggle between 1 Month and 3 Months display

Calendar Legend

The calendar uses color coding and icons to distinguish between activities:

Job Status Colors

  • Saved, Applying, Applied, Contacted
  • Interviewing, Offer, Negotiating
  • Hired, Archived

Mindfulness Activities

  • Daily Check-In
  • Gratitude Journal
  • Goal Setting
  • Rejection Reframe
  • Values Assessment

Adding Appointments

  1. Click the "+ New Appointment" button
  2. Select the desired date and time
  3. Choose the appointment type (Interview, Deadline, Follow-up)
  4. Link to a specific job application if applicable
  5. Add location details or video call information
  6. Set reminders
  7. Save your appointment

Best Practices for Calendar Use

For Interviews:

  • Add interview details immediately
  • Set preparation reminders 24 hours before
  • Include links to video calls or addresses

For Deadlines:

  • Add application deadlines when saving a job
  • Schedule follow-up reminders 1-2 weeks after applying
  • Block calendar time for application preparation

For Wellness:

  • Schedule daily check-ins at consistent times
  • Block time for goal review and reflection
  • Plan regular gratitude journaling sessions

üßò Wellness Tools

Why Wellness Matters in Job Search

Job searching can be emotionally challenging and stressful. SavvyPro JOT‚Ñ¢ offers a suite of mindfulness and wellness tools designed to help you stay positive, manage stress, and remain motivated throughout your journey.

Daily Check-In

Monitor your mental and emotional well-being during your job search.

How to Use:

  1. Select Daily Check-In from the MINDFULNESS section
  2. Choose today's date on the calendar
  3. Rate three key areas on sliders (Very Low to Very High):
    • Your mood today
    • Your energy level
    • Your stress level
  4. Add optional notes about your job search
  5. Click "Save Check-In"

Benefits:

  • Track patterns in mood and energy
  • Maintain self-awareness
  • Guide decision-making about your job search pace

Gratitude Journal

Practicing gratitude can improve your mental health and resilience.

Three Gratitude Questions:

  1. What went well in your job search today?
    • Applying to jobs
    • Networking conversations
    • Learning new skills
    • Receiving positive feedback
  2. What skills did you develop or recognize today?
    • Technical skills
    • Communication skills
    • Problem-solving abilities
  3. Who supported you in your career journey today?
    • Mentors, family, friends
    • Professional contacts

Goal Setting

Setting clear, achievable goals provides structure and motivation for your job search.

Types of Goals:

  • Short-term (Weekly): Apply to 5 jobs, update LinkedIn, reach out to contacts
  • Medium-term (Monthly): Land interviews, expand network, master a new skill
  • Long-term (3-6 months): Secure a job offer, develop expertise, build sustainable routine

SMART Framework:

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Achievable
  • Relevant
  • Time-bound

Rejection Reframe

Transform rejection into growth through mindful reflection.

The 7-Step Reframe Process:

  1. Describe the Rejection: State the objective facts
  2. Emotional Response: Process your feelings
  3. Evidence For: Identify what supports the rejection decision
  4. Evidence Against: Consider alternative perspectives
  5. Friend's Advice: Seek an outside viewpoint
  6. Lessons Learned: Extract growth opportunities
  7. Next Steps: Develop an action plan for moving forward
Pro Tip: Complete reframes within 24-48 hours of rejection, while details are fresh but emotions are manageable.

Values Assessment

Understanding your core career values helps you target opportunities that truly align with who you are.

Value Categories:

  • Work Environment: Work-Life Balance, Collaboration, Autonomy
  • Compensation & Growth: Financial Security, Growth Opportunity, Recognition
  • Purpose & Impact: Social Impact, Innovation, Creative Expression
  • Work Content: Challenge, Variety, Expertise, Leadership

How to Use Results:

  • Target companies aligned with your top values
  • Filter job opportunities based on values fit
  • Emphasize value alignment in applications
  • Prepare interview questions about company values

Premium Growth Dashboard

Track your progress across all mindfulness activities with comprehensive analytics (Professional and Executive plans).

Features:

  • Daily Check-In Overview (7-day averages)
  • Gratitude Journey tracking
  • Weekly Goals Overview
  • Reframe Journey with word clouds
  • Values Journey evolution

Accessing JOT on Mobile

Access SavvyPro JOT‚Ñ¢ from your mobile device by opening your preferred mobile web browser and navigating to app.sp-jot.com. The interface is fully responsive and automatically adapts to your screen size.

Mobile-Optimized Features

Dashboard on Mobile

  • Quick stats cards arranged vertically for easy scrolling

  • Support for swipe gestures

Board View on Mobile

Touch Interactions:

  • Tap on any job card to view full details
  • Tap and hold to drag cards between stages
  • Swipe left or right to move between pipeline stages
  • Pinch to zoom on boards with high card density

Mobile Board Tips:

  • Switch to landscape mode for increased horizontal space
  • Collapse stages you're not actively using
  • Use search and filter functions for quick access

Calendar on Mobile

  • Touch-optimized date picker
  • Swipe to move between days, weeks, or months
  • Tap appointments to view or edit details
  • Use Day or Agenda view for clearest display on smaller screens

Mindfulness Tools on Mobile

All wellness features are fully accessible:

  • Complete Daily Check-In from anywhere
  • Make quick gratitude journal entries
  • Review goals on the go
  • Access Rejection Reframe tool whenever needed

Mobile Notifications

Enable notifications for:

  • Upcoming interview reminders
  • Application deadline alerts
  • Daily check-in prompts
  • Goal progress updates

Mobile-Specific Tips

Quick Entry:

  • Use voice-to-text for quick notes and journal entries
  • Take photos of business cards after networking events
  • Screenshot job postings for offline reference

Productivity:

  • Add jobs immediately after applying from your phone
  • Update job statuses right after interviews
  • Complete daily check-ins during your commute

Organization:

  • Set phone reminders for application deadlines
  • Integrate your phone's calendar for seamless scheduling
  • Enable location-based reminders for in-person interviews

Mobile Security

Best Practices:

  • Use a strong password or enable biometric login
  • Turn on two-factor authentication when possible
  • Always log out when using shared devices
  • Keep your app updated for latest security patches

Syncing Between Devices

All changes made on mobile are instantly reflected in the desktop version and vice versa. Calendar entries, mindfulness activities, and board changes are universally tracked and synced across all devices.

Pro Tip: Take advantage of downtime by starting job applications on your mobile device, then switch to desktop for easier form completion and document uploads.

Mobile Limitations

Some tasks are best performed on desktop:

  • Bulk editing multiple jobs
  • Exporting detailed spreadsheets
  • Applying complex filters and generating reports
  • Uploading large document files
